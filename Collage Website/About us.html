About Us

We are the worlds largest Univercity

EDUFORT ACADEMY was founded with the main goal to help educational institutions by offering “all solutions under one umbrella” instead of having an employee. EDUFORT ACADEMY has been acclaimed as a leading educational research and education consulting company that offers end to end services from the commencement of your school to its effective operation in India for more than a decade. We are the pioneers in the field of Result improvement programmes for CBSE Syllabus Schools. A CBSE school affiliation is a crucial step forward in the growth of a school.

We conduct Model Examination, Unit test and Result improvement test series in CBSE schools to improve the confidence of students through practice .We also publish Teachers Manuals, Registers and Question banks . Our question papers have helped the students to score higher grades in Public examination.
